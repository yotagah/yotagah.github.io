Jump Kangaroo!

This is a mobile platformer I developed as an experiment in simplifying controls for touchscreen devices. The main challenge was designing a platform game that could be played comfortably on a phone using only two inputs.
To achieve this, the player controls a kangaroo that automatically jumps, reducing the controls to simply moving left or right by touching the corresponding side of the screen. The visual style aims for a simple cartoon look, prioritizing clarity and accessibility over detailed visuals.

Cross the Minefield

This was the first mobile game I developed. Inspired by the classic Minesweeper, the game replaces the traditional grid-revealing mechanic with a character-driven approach: the player controls a soldier who must safely cross a minefield to reach the opposite side.
The controls were adapted for touchscreen devices, focusing on simple and intuitive interactions. To gradually increase the challenge, two additional mines are added to the field with each new level, requiring more careful planning and risk management as the player progresses.
